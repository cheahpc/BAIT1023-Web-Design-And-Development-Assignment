ABOUT US

It began when the founder Angela Tsai saw the opportunity in technology related business back in 2006 after she graduated from a business school in US came and back to Malaysia. She saw the bloom in the hardware market as the demand on smart devices especially computers grow exponentially. She came to realize that it was the best time to start a company and not lay waste to the opportunity.

She set out to start a small business together with his brother who graduated from MIT. They started out with customizing computer hardware for clients and provide maintenance service. Despite the financial crisis in year 2007 and 2008, they have never strayed apart from their core value in providing the services which earned their place in their clients. Soon they have earned enough capital and registered the company in 2009. Back then the company was called Tsai Tech. The company was just a small office where technicians and some talented youngsters working days and nights providing the services. In 2010, Angela Tsai managed to pull some investor who shared the same vision and began expanding the business across Malaysia. In 2013, Tsai Tech has become the leader and tech giant in Malaysia. Along the way, the company has won a few awards and recognitions in both Malaysia and Southeast Asia. In 2014, Intel saw the value in the company and decided to sign a partnership contract with Tsai Tech.

The partnership contract with Intel brought the company to another whole new level. In 2 years, the company as expanded the business across Southeast Asia and some European countries. In 2017, the contract with Intel has ended and Tsai Tech decided to build their own processor. After a year of R&D, their very first custom hardware with their own chips and processor was released. It quickly gained momentum in the sales and a partnership was formed with Google a year later. Tsai Tech has then began developing their own software and operating system for their hardware in California. In 2020 the company released their first operating system co-developed with Google.
